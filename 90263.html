Subject Profile // Archive Entry

Marta Kessler

Born in Russia, Marta Kessler began her career in film and television at a young age, quickly establishing herself as a versatile performer with an international presence. She is best known globally for her starring role as the stubborn, brilliant Constance Contraire in the Disney+ series The Mysterious Benedict Society, as well as playing Young Jane (the younger version of Sophie Turner’s character) in the survival thriller series Survive.

With dozens of film and television projects to her name, her extensive body of work includes prominent roles in feature films and series such as the thriller Row 19, Disney’s The Santa Clauses, the sci-fi epic Cosmoball, and the horror-thriller Welcome to Mercy.

Beyond acting, she maintains an active presence in modeling and digital media, collaborating with brands and causes that align with her values. This archive documents some of her professional trajectory, campaign work, and ongoing creative output.
