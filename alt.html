How to Use This App

  1. Choose a character (X, XX, or XXX) you want to write about.
  2. Write your entry in the corresponding text area.
  3. Click the "Jot" button to save your entry.
  4. Your entry will appear in the corresponding column below.
  5. Entries are automatically saved and will persist even if you close the browser.
  6. Use the "Clear All Entries" button to delete all entries (use with caution).

Happy writing!
