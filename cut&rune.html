    RUNIC CODEX

    These runes draw from two strains of history:

    • Elder Futhark: The oldest runic alphabet (ca. 150–800 CE)
      • ᛀ: Represents the beginning, creation, primal force
      • ᛐ: Symbolizes knowledge, wisdom, hidden knowledge
      • ᛔ: Represents crossroads, choice, turning point
    • Younger Futhark: Viking Age alphabet (ca. 800–1100 CE)
      • ᛪ: Represents strength, resilience, enduring power
      • ᛥ: Symbolizes communication, connection, spoken word
      • ᛨ: Represents nature, growth, cycle of life

    Originally inscribed on stone, wood, and metal for divination and communication. In modern times, runes serve as symbols of ancient wisdom and are used in art, literature, and mystical practice.
