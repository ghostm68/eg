“remember when you were mine” is the haunting refrain from, HOLLYWOODLAND. The song, by TWO SKINNY GIRLS, reminds us of the golden age of cinema — via a look at the post modern anxiety of women in the city. Dreamlike yet not at all complicated....

It literally fixes the audiences memory by a gentle reminder....

There are love songs but this one goes beyond the call of duty.
